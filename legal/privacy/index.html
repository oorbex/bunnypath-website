Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026
Effective date: April 19, 2026

BunnyPath ("we," "us," or "our") is a parent-facing tool that surfaces a curated catalog of play ideas. The app is designed to be operated by parents and guardians — not by children directly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information. When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials (or tokens from Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In). We do not collect or store credit card numbers.
  • Household Profile Fields. You may enter a first name or nickname and an age range for each child in your household to help the app surface age-appropriate activities. These are optional parent-supplied labels used only inside your own account — we do not solicit information directly from any child.
  • Activity Preferences. You may tell us about the kinds of activities and focus areas your household is interested in to help personalize suggestions.
  • User-Submitted Content. You may optionally upload photos of completed activities or provide written feedback and ratings.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data. We collect information about how you interact with the app, including features used, activities viewed or saved, session duration, and device type.
  • Device Information. We collect your device model, operating system version, app version, and a unique device identifier for push notification delivery.
  • Crash and Performance Data. We collect anonymized crash logs and performance metrics to improve app stability.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect last names, physical addresses, schools, photos, voice recordings, or precise geolocation about any child.
  • We do not allow children to use the app directly. BunnyPath is designed to be operated by a parent or guardian.
  • We do not share child-related data with any third-party AI service. Activity matching happens against our curated catalog inside our own backend.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Personalize Activity Suggestions. Your child's age and preferences are used to surface age-appropriate, interest-driven play ideas from our curated catalog.
  • Improve the App. Aggregated and anonymized usage data helps us understand which features are most helpful and develop new functionality.
  • Communicate With You. We use your email address to send transactional messages and, if you opt in, promotional content.
  • Provide Customer Support. If you contact us, we use your information to respond to your request.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

BunnyPath is a tool for parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All child-related data (first name or nickname, age range, activity history) is provided by the parent and used only within the parent's account.

Key Protections

  • Adult-Only Gate. Only adults (age 18 or older) may create accounts. The app is not intended to be operated by children.
  • Parent-Provided Data. Any child-related fields (first name or nickname, age range) are entered by the parent. We never solicit information directly from a child.
  • Minimal Data. We store only the minimum information needed to surface age-appropriate activities from our curated catalog.
  • No Child-Directed Advertising. We do not tag the app as child-directed for ad networks. We do not use child-related data for profiling, behavioral targeting, or cross-context advertising.
  • No Sharing for Marketing. We never sell, rent, or share child-related information with third parties for their marketing purposes.
  • Parent Controls. Parents may review, correct, or delete any child-related information at any time from Settings, or by contacting support@bunnypath.com.

4. Activity Catalog

BunnyPath's activity catalog is curated by our team, reviewed before publication, and published in periodic batches. It is never generated in real time from your child's data.

  • No Third-Party AI Calls. The app does not send your child's name, age, interests, or any other information to any external AI service.
  • Matching Happens Locally. Activities are filtered and ranked using your preferences inside our Supabase backend — not by any third-party large language model.
  • Content Review. Activities pass through our internal review before being added to the catalog. Always use your judgment and supervise your child during activities.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Infrastructure. Your data is stored on Supabase, a secure cloud database platform.
  • Encryption. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2 or higher).
  • Access Controls. Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis with row-level security policies.
  • Authentication. We support Apple Sign-In, Google Sign-In, and email-based authentication. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and never stored in plain text.
  • Incident Response. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities in accordance with applicable law.

6. Third-Party Services

BunnyPath integrates with the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, and storage.
  • PostHog — Anonymized product analytics.
  • Google AdMob — Contextual ads only; no behavioral targeting and no use of child-related data.
  • Qonversion — Subscription and billing management (works with the Apple App Store and Google Play Store).
  • Apple Sign-In — Authentication (Apple manages credentials).
  • Google Sign-In — Authentication (Google manages credentials).
  • Amazon SES — Transactional and marketing email.
  • Amazon SNS — Push notifications.

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts. We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide our services.
  • Deleted Accounts. When you delete your account, we will permanently delete your personal data and all associated child profiles within 30 days. Account deletion is available directly in the app under Settings.
  • Backup Systems. Data in backup systems may persist for up to an additional 30 days after deletion.
  • Legal Requirements. We may retain certain information as required by law even after account deletion.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access. Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you and your child.
  • Correction. Request that we correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion. Request that we delete your personal data and your child's data. You can delete your account directly in the app at any time.
  • Export. Request a portable copy of your data in a commonly used format.
  • Opt Out. Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Withdraw Consent. Withdraw your consent for processing at any time.

9. Push Notifications and Email

  • Push Notifications. You may opt in to receive push notifications for daily activity reminders, milestone celebrations, and app updates. You can disable these at any time through your device settings.
  • Transactional Email. We send emails for account-related purposes (verification, password resets, subscription confirmations). These cannot be opted out of while your account is active.
  • Marketing Email. With your consent, we may send promotional emails. You can unsubscribe at any time via the "unsubscribe" link or in Settings.

10. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers and service providers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in compliance with applicable laws.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by email before the changes take effect. Your continued use of BunnyPath after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@bunnypath.com.

For COPPA-related inquiries or to exercise parental rights, please include "COPPA Request" in the subject line of your email.
