Rainbow Sponge Stamps
15 min Ages 2–5y No mess
About

Create vibrant rainbow art using kitchen sponges cut into fun shapes. Kids dip, stamp, and layer colors to make one-of-a-kind masterpieces.

Materials
Kitchen sponges Washable paint Paper plates Card stock
Steps
  1. Cut sponges into shapes — stars, circles, hearts, or squares.
  2. Pour different paint colors onto paper plates.
  3. Dip a sponge shape into paint and press firmly onto the card stock.
